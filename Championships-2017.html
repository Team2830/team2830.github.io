Championships

For the FRC Championship, Team 2830 will be in the Daly Division. Daly Division's namesake, Marie M. Daly (1921-2003), was a famous scientist, best known for being the first African American woman to get a Ph.D. in chemistry. Throughout her career, Daly researched the causes of heart attacks and taught biochemistry courses at various institutions.

FIRST® (For Inspiration and Recognition of Science and Technology) is a STEM program. Founded by Dean Kamen and Woodie Flowers in 1992, FIRST® aims to inspire students of all ages to pursue careers in science and technology. FIRST® consists of four competition series of varying calibers: FIRST® LEGO® League Jr.(Kindergarten - Grade 4), FIRST® LEGO® League (Grades 4-8), FIRST® Tech Challenge (Grades 7-12), and FIRST® Robotics Competition (Grades 9-12). We are part of FRC, which currently consists of 5,869 teams.
