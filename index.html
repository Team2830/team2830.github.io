THE ROBOTIGERS ARE HEADED TO THE WORLD CHAMPIONSHIPS!

The event happens just next week in St. Louis, MO. What does this mean for the team? It means that the students, coaches, and mentors have put in a lot of work so far this year, but they can't take a break just yet. We have just over a week to build a crate, ship the robot to St. Louis, secure team lodging and transportation, get all of our paperwork filled out, rearrange our schedules, and more importantly, raise funds to cover the cost of the trip for the team. The Robotigers are a community-based team, and we believe that our members' financial situations should not be a limiting factor in their attendance at championships.
How can I help? Click the Donate Button below, or if you would prefer to send a check, please reach out to us for further details. Please note, we are affiliated with a 501(c)(3) organization, and will be doing our best to provide receipts in a timely manner. Our team's goal is to raise $7500. This will ensure that we are able to cover the cost of transportation, hotel rooms, and meals for the team. Please stay tuned for updates! And thanks for all of your support!
