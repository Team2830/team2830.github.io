World Championships!

The RoboTigers want to go back to the WORLD CHAMPIONSHIPS this year held in Detroit Michigan!

The students, coaches, and mentors are putting in a lot of work to get ready for the 2019 competitions, but we can't do it alone. We need to secure team lodging and transportation for our upcoming regionals. The RoboTigers are a community-based team, and we believe that our members' financial situations should not be a limiting factor in their attendance at these events.
How can I help? Click the Donate Button below, or if you would prefer to send a check, please reach out to us for further details. Please note, we are affiliated with a 501(c)(3) organization, and will be doing our best to provide receipts in a timely manner. Please stay tuned for updates! And thanks for all of your support!
